NDS/DS tension ratios.

Why tension balance matters

Wheel strength scales with NDS tension. Equilibrium: T_DS × sin β_DS = T_NDS × sin β_NDS. The 3 mm offset improves the ratio by ~14–15 pp.

Spoke diameter effect

EA = E × π(d/2)². Larger spokes increase stiffness but do not change tension ratio — ratio depends only on geometry (bracing angles). Stiffness and strength scale with EA.
